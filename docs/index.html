Built for Developers

Everything you'd want from a physical contribution display — nothing you wouldn't.

⚡

5-Minute Setup

Plug in, open the app, connect to WiFi, enter your GitHub username. That's it. No soldering, no config files, no terminal.

🔌

Always Current

Auto-refreshes your contribution data throughout the day. Push a commit and see it reflected on your display within minutes.

🎨

8 Color Themes

Classic GitHub green, blue, purple, orange, pink, cyan, white, or GitHub's own subdued palette. Switch themes anytime from the app.

👥

Multi-User Rotation

Add up to 5 GitHub usernames and the display cycles between them. Perfect for teams or showing off your org.

🔒

Private & Secure

No accounts, no cloud service, no subscriptions. Your data goes straight from GitHub to your display over your own WiFi. Nothing passes through our servers.

🔄

OTA Updates

Firmware updates delivered automatically over WiFi. New features and fixes without plugging anything in.
